AI TRANSPARENCY

a personal statement on how AI is used here

Draft — written to be personalized. Edit this to your own voice and stance before relying on it publicly.

I believe people deserve to know when and how artificial intelligence is involved in the things they use. The Reading Room is built and maintained with the help of AI tools, and this page explains what that means — plainly, and without spin.

How AI is used

  • Building the app. I use AI coding assistants (Anthropic's Claude) to help write, review, and refactor the code that runs this site.
  • With human oversight. AI is a tool, not the author. I read, test, and make the final call on every change that ships. Decisions about features, data, and how your information is handled are mine.
  • Not pretending to be human. Nothing on this site impersonates a person. If a feature is automated, it's automated.

What this means for your data

  • Your account is created through Google sign-in. The app stores your display name, avatar, the clubs you join, your reading progress, your reactions, and your reviews.
  • Your reading data powers the spoiler protection: reactions are hidden from anyone who hasn't read that far. That gating is enforced on the server, not just in the page.
  • Your content is shared with the members of your club, by design. It is not sold, and it is not used to train AI models.

My commitments

  • Be honest about where AI is involved.
  • Keep a human responsible for what the app does.
  • Collect only the data the app needs to work.
  • Update this page when those practices change.
