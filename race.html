THE MARBLE RACE

a book club edition

The rules, briefly

  1. Create a marble for every book-club member. Give them a name, a picture if you like, and a theme song (YouTube link).
  2. Start a race. Pick which marbles are competing today and choose a map.
  3. Watch the physics. Orbs will spawn during the race — some help, some hurt.
  4. The marble in the lead has its theme song playing. When the lead changes, so does the music.
  5. First marble to the finish picks the next book.
